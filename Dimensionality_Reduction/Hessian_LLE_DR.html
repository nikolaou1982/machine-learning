Computation Tip

Hessian LLE requires k > d(d+1)/2 + d neighbors (e.g., k > 5 for 2D, k > 9 for 3D) and involves computing local tangent spaces and Hessians. Fast Approximation Mode uses landmarks to speed up computation for large datasets (> 200 samples) with minimal accuracy loss.
